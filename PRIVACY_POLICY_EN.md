# Privacy Policy - Sandak App

**Last Updated:** November 27, 2025

---

## Introduction

**Sandak** ("we," "us," "the app") provides vehicle insurance services electronically by collecting necessary information and providing insurance services to users through the app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data, using it in compliance with local and international laws and regulations.

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when using the Sandak app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide vehicle insurance services effectively, including:

### A. Personal Information
- Full name
- Phone number
- Email address
- National ID number (when required for insurance procedures)
- Date of birth (for age verification and insurance requirements)

### B. Vehicle Information
- Vehicle type and model
- Year of manufacture
- License plate number
- Chassis number (VIN) - if required by insurance company
- Vehicle color
- Approximate vehicle value

### C. Documents and Images

We may request upload of the following images and documents:
- Driver's license photo
- Vehicle registration (Istimara) photo
- Vehicle photos (for evaluation or comprehensive insurance)
- National ID card photo
- Any other documents required by the insurance company

**Important Note:** We use Artificial Intelligence technology (Google Gemini AI) to automatically extract data from uploaded documents, aiming to speed up data entry and reduce errors. These documents are processed securely and are not retained by the AI service.

### D. Technical Information

We automatically collect some technical information to improve performance and security:
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- IP address
- Usage data within the app (such as pages viewed, time spent)
- Session and authentication information
- Error logs and technical issues

### E. Location Information (Optional)

**The app does not currently collect geographical location information.** We may request access to your location in the future to provide additional services (such as nearby service centers), and this will only be done with your explicit consent.

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

### A. Service Delivery
- Create and verify user accounts
- Provide vehicle insurance application services
- Process and manage insurance applications
- Issue price quotes from insurance companies
- Send notifications about application status

### B. Communication
- Send order confirmations and notifications
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Send important updates about the app or service
- Inform you about new offers (with your consent)

### C. Improvement and Development
- Analyze app usage to improve performance
- Develop new features based on user needs
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Conduct research and analysis (aggregated, non-personal data)

### D. Security and Compliance
- Protect accounts from fraud and unauthorized use
- Comply with laws and regulatory obligations
- Respond to legal requests from authorized entities

## 3. Sharing Information with Third Parties

**We never sell, trade, or rent user data.**

Your data may only be shared in the following cases:

### A. Insurance Companies
- To process insurance applications and issue policies
- To evaluate the vehicle and determine insurance value
- To conduct inspections or process claims

### B. Technical Service Providers
- **Firebase (Google):** For database hosting, file storage, and authentication
- **Google Gemini AI:** For processing and extracting data from documents (temporarily only)
- **Expo:** For app management and distribution

All these providers are committed to data protection and do not use it for other purposes.

### C. Legal and Government Entities
- When there is an official request from an authorized judicial or government entity
- To comply with local laws
- To protect our legal rights or user safety

### D. In Case of Ownership Transfer
- In case of company sale, merger, or ownership transfer, data may be transferred to the new owner with commitment to the same privacy policy

## 4. Data Storage and Protection

### Data Retention Period
- We retain your data throughout your use of the app
- After account deletion, data is retained for **180 days** for legal and insurance obligations
- After that, personal data is permanently deleted (except legally required data)

### Protection Measures
We use a comprehensive set of technical and organizational measures:

#### Technical Protection:
- **Encryption:** Data encryption during transmission (SSL/TLS) and during storage
- **Secure Authentication:** Using Firebase Authentication with password encryption
- **Firewalls:** Protection of servers and databases
- **Backup:** Regular data backup
- **Security Monitoring:** Continuous monitoring for suspicious activities

#### Organizational Protection:
- Limiting employee access to sensitive information (access on need basis only)
- Employee training on data protection
- Strict information security policies
- Regular review of cybersecurity procedures

**Note:** Despite all measures, 100% security cannot be guaranteed for any electronic system. We recommend not sharing your account information with anyone.
